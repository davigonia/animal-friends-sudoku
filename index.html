Animal Friends 🐰

How to Play Animal Friends! 🌟

1. Pick your favorite difficulty:

  • Easy: 3x3 grid with 3 animals
  • Medium: 4x4 grid with 4 animals
  • Hard: 5x5 grid with 5 animals

2. The Rules are Simple:

  • Each animal can only appear ONCE in each row
  • Each animal can only appear ONCE in each column
  • Help all animal friends find their perfect spot! 🎈

3. Need Help?

  • Use hints to reveal one correct animal (3 hints per game)
  • You have 5 hearts - be careful not to lose them all!
  • Try to beat your best time! ⏰
